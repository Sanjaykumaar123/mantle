## How to Use

### Step 1: Start the Platform
```bash
cd rwaOS-mvp
docker-compose up
```

This will start:
- ✅ Frontend at http://localhost:3000
- ✅ Backend at http://localhost:8080
- ✅ PostgreSQL at localhost:5432 (database: aegisai_backend)

### Step 2: Access the Intelligence Center
1. Log in with any built-in user:
   - Username: `operator_user`
   - Or connect with MetaMask on Arbitrum Sepolia

2. Click **⭐ Intelligence Center** in the sidebar

3. Explore:
   - Executive Intelligence Dashboard
   - Asset Intelligence (per-asset analysis)
   - Compliance Intelligence (violation detection)
   - Disclosure Intelligence (scope recommendations)
   - Audit Intelligence (confidence scoring)
   - Institutional Risk Center (multi-dimensional risk)

### Step 3: Deploy
See `QUICKSTART.md` for production deployment instructions.
